Arcanine changes

  • In Generation 8, Hisuian Arcanine has Justified as its hidden ability.
  • In Generation 1, Arcanine has a base Special stat of 80.
  • In Generations 1-4, Arcanine has a base experience yield of 213.
  • In Generations 2-7, Arcanine has a base Friendship value of 70.
